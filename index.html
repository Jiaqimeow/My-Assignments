Openrice Restaurant Ranking Based on Data Analysis

As a student who lives in Tai Wai, I appreciate the convenience brought about by abundant food options very much. But sometimes it's hard to find the one that suits your taste, especially when you need to balance the food and price.

In this assignment, I summarized the top 10 welcomed restaurants that are suitable for students, and the top 5 welcomed cuisine styles which represent the food style of Tai Wai based on data scraping, cleaning, analysis and presentation. The results are shown on the right. Hope it could give you an idea of what to eat in Tai Wai during leisure times.
